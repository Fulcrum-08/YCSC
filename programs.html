Our Programs

Practical solutions to reduce emissions, lower energy costs, and improve comfort for homes and communities.

Available Programs

Heat Pumps

High-efficiency electric heating and cooling systems.

Heat pumps move heat instead of creating it, making them one of the most energy-efficient technologies available.

  • Provide heating and cooling
  • Reduce fossil fuel use
  • Lower long-term energy costs
  • Eligible for rebates and incentives

Air Sealing

Reduces drafts and energy loss throughout a home.

Air sealing targets leaks around doors, windows, attics, and basements to keep conditioned air inside.

  • Improves comfort
  • Reduces heating and cooling demand
  • Works well with insulation upgrades

Insulation Upgrades

Keeps homes warm in winter and cool in summer.

Insulation upgrades prevent heat transfer and reduce energy waste year-round.

  • Attic, wall, and basement insulation
  • Lower utility bills
  • Long-lasting energy savings

Solar Energy

Clean, renewable electricity from the sun.

Solar panels reduce dependence on the grid and provide long-term clean energy.

  • Lower electricity costs
  • Reduce carbon footprint
  • Net metering options available

Home Energy Audits

Identify inefficiencies and energy-saving opportunities.

Energy audits assess insulation, air leakage, heating systems, and appliances.

  • Personalized upgrade recommendations
  • Improves comfort and efficiency
  • First step toward electrification
